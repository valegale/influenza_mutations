Co-occurring mutations in influenza virus

Analysis of the mutations occurring from flu season 09/10 to flu season 10/11

Co-occurring mutation network

The network includes all mutations with minimum support >=0.05 and minimum confidence >= 0.5. Each pink box represents a rule. Support, Confidence, and Zhang's metric can be visualized by pointing the cursor directly on the node.
Each mutation is a node and the support for the individual mutation can be visualized by pointing the cursor on the node. HA and NA mutation nodes have different colors.
Mutations in the antigenic regions have a colored border around the node.
Each rule has directed edges coming out and in, according to the direction of the rule. The thickness of the edge indicates the support of the rule and rules with a high Zhang's metric (likely to be associated) are indicated with a green line.
